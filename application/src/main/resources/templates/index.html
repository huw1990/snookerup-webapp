HOW IT WORKS

BROWSE

Search our vast catalogue of routines for a routine that aims to improve part of the game you struggle with

PLAY

Go to your local club and try out your selected routine a few times

RECORD

After each attempt, add your score to your SnookerUp profile (don't worry about mistakes, scores are private to yourself!)

ANALYSE

Keep practising the routines, and over time you'll see your progress and how your scores improve!

SAMPLE ROUTINE

Clearing the Colours

Put all colours on their spots, then try to clear them in order, i.e. yellow, green, brown, blue, pink, black.

Try a variation to change the difficulty of this routine:

  • As a loop: Complete the routine, but with your last shot of the routine, play position for the first shot again, then carry on.
  • Limit the number of cushions you can hit: Try to complete the routine either without hitting a routine at all, or with at most a fixed number of cushions, e.g. 3, 5, or 7.
